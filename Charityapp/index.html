About Us Sarvodaya Vichar Mandal

The mission of the society is to actively engage in the nation building process through promoting the principles of Gandhian Ideology and the ideas enshrined in the Constitution of India.

Vision

Our vision is to be an ideological organisation which will be the vanguard to defend and preserve the idea of India from the onslaught of divisive ideologies and policies of various groups and governments.
